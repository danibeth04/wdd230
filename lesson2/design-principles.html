White Space and Clean Design

Zen Habits

zenhabits.net

The Zen Habits website is a great example of the use of white space and clean design. On mobile, the design is minimalist, monochromatic, and clean. There is little to no extraneous design, drawing the user's attention simply to the short blog post.

Contrast

Andy Warhol

waaarhol.com

The Andy Warhol site is a great example of contrast on the mobile app. The image above shows a bright pink background with bright yellow text. The combination of colors is high-contrast without being painful to look at.

Repetition

Zillow

zillow.com

The above screenshot of the mobile Zillow website shows strong repetions. The images used for each section are similar, and the fonts and layout of each section repeat.
