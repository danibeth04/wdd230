Description of Target Audience

The target audience for this site is small business owners and individuals who are interested in community development.

Personas

  • Molly: a mid-30s stay-at-home mom. She likes being involved in her community and keeping up-to-date on any local events, and she has a vested interest in community development.
  • Tim: a local business owner. He wants to network with other business owners and get the word out about his own business.

Scenarios

  • When does school start?
  • When/where is the next festival going to be held?
  • How can I register my business for a booth at the next festival?
  • When/where will the next B2B meeting be held?
  • What is the timeline for the new town aquatic center?
