About Bountiful Foods

Bountiful Foods was established in 2020. Our mission arose out of a desire to provide a locally-sourced option for organically produced fruits and vegetables.

Our flagship product, the Fresh Smoothie, allows customers to customize their own fruit drink using the best local produce around!

Find us at our downtown Carlsbad location, 111 E. Main St., or visit any one of our foodtrucks.
